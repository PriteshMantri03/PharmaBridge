About Pharma Bridge

Pharma Bridge is dedicated to connecting pharmaceutical companies with NGOs to ensure that no medicine goes to waste. Our mission is to bridge the gap in healthcare by turning excess into access, providing essential medicines to those in need.

Our Mission

We aim to create a sustainable and efficient system for medicine donation and distribution, leveraging technology to track and manage requests and deliveries in real-time.
